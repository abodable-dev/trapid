# TRAPID - Executive Summary

Trapid is an all-in-one construction management platform that manages your entire business from quote to completion. Unlike fragmented tools and spreadsheets, Trapid provides one integrated system with AI-powered automation, real-time financial tracking, and seamless integrations with Xero, Microsoft 365, and Twilio. Built specifically for Australian builders, Trapid eliminates administrative chaos and protects profit margins through intelligent cost control and supplier management.
